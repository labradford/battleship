Game Rules:

Travel across the sea on your Battleship looking for enemy ships. There is a total of 8 enemy ships. One ship that is five squares long, two ships each that are four, three and two squares in length, and one ship that is a single square. You have 50 torpedos to use. Ships will not be placed adjacent to each other. Click a square to begin your adventure. Good Luck Sailor!
